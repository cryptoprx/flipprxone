### What is FLIPPRX ONE?
FLIPPRX ONE is a next-generation, self-custodial wallet for the XRP Ledger (XRPL). It combines advanced security features like SnapTap WebAuth with comprehensive functionality including trading, NFT management, and encrypted messaging.

### Is FLIPPRX ONE free to use?
Yes! FLIPPRX ONE is completely free to use. You only pay standard XRPL network fees for transactions (typically fractions of a cent).

### Do I need to download anything?
No. FLIPPRX ONE runs entirely in your web browser. No downloads or installations required.

### What makes FLIPPRX ONE different from other wallets?
- **SnapTap Security** - Dual-key authentication with NFC/biometric
- **Micro LP Trading** - Built-in DeFi access
- **MIMO Messenger** - Encrypted messaging on XRPL
- **NFT Gallery** - Complete NFT management
- **100% Self-Custodial** - You control your keys
- **Open Source** - Transparent and auditable

### Is FLIPPRX ONE open source?
Yes! Our code is open source and available for review on GitHub.

### Which platforms are supported?
FLIPPRX ONE works on:
- üíª Desktop browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

- üåê Any device with a modern web browser

## üöÄ Getting Started

### How do I create a wallet?
1. Visit FLIPPRX ONE
2. Click "Create New Wallet"
3. Choose SnapTap or password authentication
4. Write down your seed phrase
5. Verify your backup
6. Start using your wallet!

### Can I import an existing wallet?
Yes! FLIPPRX ONE supports multiple import methods:
- 12/24 word seed phrases (BIP39)
- Family seeds (starts with 's')
- Xaman secret numbers (8x6 grid)
- Both ed25519 and secp256k1 keys

### What is a seed phrase?
A seed phrase is a list of 12 or 24 words that can restore your entire wallet. It's your master backup - keep it safe and never share it with anyone.

### Do I need an account to use FLIPPRX ONE?
No! FLIPPRX ONE is completely self-custodial. No accounts, no registration, no personal information required.

### What is SnapTap?
SnapTap is our revolutionary dual-key authentication system that combines device security with NFC or biometric authentication for maximum protection.

## üîí Security & Privacy

### Is FLIPPRX ONE secure?
Yes! FLIPPRX ONE uses:
- AES-256-GCM encryption (military-grade)
- Client-side only architecture
- No server-side key storage
- SnapTap dual-key authentication
- Open source code for transparency

### Can FLIPPRX ONE access my funds?
No. FLIPPRX ONE is 100% self-custodial. Your private keys are encrypted and stored only on your device. We cannot access your funds under any circumstances.

### What if I lose my seed phrase?
Unfortunately, if you lose your seed phrase and lose access to your device, your funds cannot be recovered. This is the nature of self-custodial wallets. Always keep secure backups!

### Where are my private keys stored?
Your private keys are encrypted and stored locally in your browser's secure storage. They never leave your device and are never transmitted to any server.

### What data does FLIPPRX ONE collect?
None! We don't collect:
- Private keys or seed phrases
- Personal information
- Transaction history
- Usage analytics
- IP addresses
- Any identifying data

### How do I keep my wallet secure?
**Best Practices:**
- ‚úÖ Write down your seed phrase on paper
- ‚úÖ Store backups in multiple secure locations
- ‚úÖ Use SnapTap authentication
- ‚úÖ Keep your device secure
- ‚úÖ Verify addresses before sending
- ‚ùå Never share your seed phrase
- ‚ùå Never store seed phrase digitally

### What is the difference between SnapTap and password?
- **SnapTap**: Dual-key authentication requiring both device + NFC/biometric. Maximum security, phishing-resistant.
- **Password**: Traditional password-based encryption. Simpler but less secure than SnapTap.

## üíº Wallet Management

### How do I view my balance?
Your XRP and token balances are displayed on the main dashboard. They update automatically every 10 seconds, or you can refresh manually.

### What is XRP reserve?
XRPL requires a minimum balance (reserve) to maintain an account. Currently:
- Base reserve: 1 XRP
- Per-trustline: 0.2 XRP
- Per-object: 0.2 XRP

This reserve is locked but still yours - it's not a fee.

### How do I add tokens to my wallet?
1. Go to Trustline Manager
2. Select the token you want to add
3. Click "Create Trustline"
4. Approve the transaction
5. The token will appear in your balance

### Can I have multiple wallets?
Yes! You can create or import multiple wallets and switch between them easily.

### How do I backup my wallet?
Your seed phrase IS your backup. Write it down on paper and store it securely. You can restore your entire wallet using just the seed phrase.

### Can I change my password?
Yes, but this creates a new encryption layer. Your seed phrase remains the same and can always restore your wallet.

## üí∏ Transactions

### How do I send XRP?
1. Click "Send"
2. Enter recipient address
3. Enter amount
4. Review details
5. Authenticate with SnapTap or password
6. Confirm transaction

### How do I send tokens?
Same as XRP, but select the token from the dropdown menu. Make sure the recipient has a trustline for that token.

### What are transaction fees?
XRPL fees are extremely low, typically 0.00001 XRP (a fraction of a cent). FLIPPRX ONE automatically calculates the optimal fee.

### How long do transactions take?
XRPL transactions typically confirm in 3-5 seconds. You'll see confirmation almost immediately.

### Can I cancel a transaction?
No. Once a transaction is submitted to the XRPL network, it cannot be cancelled or reversed. Always double-check before confirming!

### What is a destination tag?
A destination tag is an additional identifier used by exchanges and services to route payments to specific accounts. Always include it when sending to exchanges.

### Where can I see my transaction history?
Click on "History" in the main menu to view all your past transactions with full details.

## üîÑ Trading & DeFi

### What is Micro LP trading?
Micro LP allows you to provide liquidity to XRPL automated market maker (AMM) pools and earn trading fees.

### Which pools are supported?
Currently:
- FLIPPRX/XRP
- FCP/XRP
- MFLIP/XRP

More pools coming soon!

### How do I add liquidity?
1. Go to "Micro LP" section
2. Select a pool
3. Enter amount to deposit
4. Review pool share and LP tokens
5. Confirm transaction
6. Receive LP tokens

### How do I remove liquidity?
1. Go to your LP positions
2. Select pool to withdraw from
3. Enter amount or percentage
4. Confirm transaction
5. Receive underlying tokens

### What are LP tokens?
LP (Liquidity Provider) tokens represent your share of a liquidity pool. They can be redeemed for your portion of the pool plus earned fees.

### How do I earn from LP?
When traders swap tokens in the pool, they pay a small fee. This fee is distributed proportionally to all LP token holders.

### Can I swap tokens directly?
Yes! Use the swap feature to trade tokens directly through AMM pools without providing liquidity.

## üé® NFTs & Domains

### How do I view my NFTs?
Click on "NFT Gallery" to see all your XRPL NFTs with images and metadata.

### What are on-chain domains?
On-chain domains are human-readable identifiers stored as NFTs on XRPL. They make sharing your address easier.

### Who can get a domain?
Domains are available to FLIPPRX holders (2M+ community members) as an exclusive feature.

### How do I claim a domain?
1. Go to "Domains" section
2. Check availability
3. Enter desired domain name
4. Confirm registration
5. Domain appears in your NFT gallery

### Can I transfer my domain?
Yes! Domains are NFTs and can be transferred like any other NFT.

### Do domains expire?
No. Once claimed, domains are yours permanently (as long as you hold the NFT).

## üí¨ MIMO Messenger

### What is MIMO?
MIMO is an end-to-end encrypted messaging system built directly on XRPL. Messages are stored on the blockchain and only readable by sender and recipient.

### Is MIMO free?
Yes, but messages require a small XRPL transaction fee (fractions of a cent).

### How do I send a message?
1. Go to MIMO Messenger
2. Select or add a contact
3. Type your message
4. Send (requires transaction approval)
5. Message encrypted and stored on XRPL

### Are messages really private?
Yes! Messages are encrypted end-to-end. Only you and your recipient can read them. Even we cannot decrypt your messages.

### Can I delete messages?
Messages on XRPL are permanent, but you can enable self-destructing messages for future conversations.

### How do I add contacts?
Enter their XRPL address or domain name to add them to your contact list.

## üîß Troubleshooting

### My balance isn't updating
Try these steps:
1. Click the refresh button
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear browser cache
4. Reload the page
5. Verify you're connected to the correct network

### Transaction failed - what happened?
Common reasons:
- Insufficient balance (including reserves)
- Invalid destination address
- Missing trustline
- Network congestion
- Incorrect destination tag

Check the error message for specific details.

### I can't connect my wallet
Possible solutions:
1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser
3. Check if JavaScript is enabled
4. Disable conflicting browser extensions
5. Verify your device meets requirements

### SnapTap isn't working
Troubleshooting:
1. Ensure your device supports WebAuth
2. Check NFC is enabled (if using NFC)
3. Try biometric authentication instead
4. Update your browser
5. Use password authentication as fallback

### I forgot my password
If you have your seed phrase:
1. Create a new wallet
2. Import using your seed phrase
3. Set a new password

Without your seed phrase, funds cannot be recovered.
